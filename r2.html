Six of Crows

Author: Leigh Bardugo

Publication Date: Feb 1, 2018

$199.45

It is a truth universally acknowledged that everyone loves a good heist. And indeed, that's what we get in one of the most talked-about books online, SIX OF CROWS. Part of Leigh Bardugo’s popular Grishaverse, the novel follows the crew brought together by Kaz Brekker, a criminal mastermind looking to cash in on the biggest job of his career . . . provided his team can keep from killing each other first.

About the Author

Leigh Bardugo is a renowned author known for his work in the genre. He has written several bestselling novels and has a large following of readers who appreciate his storytelling and writing style.
