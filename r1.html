Yellow Wife

Author:Sadeqa Johnson

Publication Date: January 3, 2020

$69.99

On Pheby Delores Brown’s eighteenth birthday, she hopes to get her freedom, allowing her to finally leave the plantation she grew up on, and be with Essex Henry, another slave who is looking to escape. Those dreams are horrifyingly shattered when Pheby is betrayed and sold to a notorious jail owner, and things only get harder from there.

About the Author

Sadeqa Johnson is a renowned author known for his work in the genre. He has written several bestselling novels and has a large following of readers who appreciate his storytelling and writing style.
